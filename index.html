Career Opportunities

Explore a multitude of exciting career opportunities in the field of Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering. Unleash your potential as you work on diverse tasks, from design and marketing to testing, manufacturing, and research. Whether in the field, labs, or offices, you'll utilize cutting-edge technology, engaging with people directly or electronically. Join us on a path of innovation, growth, and meaningful impact.

  • Agricultural and Biosystems Engineer

    They delve into a plethora of dynamic roles, spanning from design and marketing to testing and research. They expertly navigate the fields, whether on-site, in laboratories, or within offices, utilizing cutting-edge technology to drive experiments and designs while fostering connections with people.

  • Design Engineer

    Design Engineers shape ideas into functional prototypes using cost-effective and efficient processes. Their responsibilities encompass crafting purpose-driven design briefs, exploring production methods, and employing engineering software to refine and evolve their daily operations, products, and organization.

  • Test Engineer

    Test Engineers ensure systems and products meet business needs and perform flawlessly. With a keen eye for detail, they conduct comprehensive tests covering aspects like performance, service, regression, components, and functions, collaborating closely with development teams for seamless version management.

  • Project Engineer

    Project Engineers oversee technical and engineering projects with finesse. Unlike project managers, they approach tasks from an engineering standpoint, working closely with stakeholders, managing budgets, planning, and personnel to ensure precise adherence to technical requirements.

  • Irrigation Engineer

    Irrigation Engineers tackle large-scale and intricate landscape projects, often requiring approval from engineers or landscape architects. Through thorough site analysis and research, they address structural and environmental challenges to bring efficient irrigation solutions to life.

  • Soil scientist

    Soil Scientists conduct in-depth research and experiments on various soil and plant types to assess soil capabilities and the impact of alternative practices on productivity. Their expertise spans multiple scientific fields, including agronomy, soil science, forestry, and agriculture.

About Us

The Philippine Society of Agricultural and Biosystems Engineers Pre-professional Group(PSABE-PPG) of Southern Philippines Agri-business and Marine and Aquatic School ofTechnology (SPAMAST) chapter under the Institute of Agricultural TechnologyandEntrepreneurial Studies (IATES) is a non-governmental organization comprising studentsstudying Bachelor of Science in Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering (BSABE) enrolled at SPAMAST.

Our Goals

  • To foster critical thinking, leadership, and influence, particularly in the fields of engineering, technology, and agriculture.

  • To provide exceptional research and training in the realm of agricultural and biosystemsengineering.

  • To guide pre-professionals with the ethics, principles, and values of agricultural andbiosystems engineering.

Our Mission

To provide inclusive education and advanced studies in creating and molding future agricultural and biosystems engineers in the application of Agricultural and Biosystems Engineeringprinciples, values, projects, and programs.

Our Vision

A center of excellence and grandeur for providing cutting-edge education and guidance for innovative engineering sciences and technologies to sustainably.
